MSc Interactive Media Thesis

My MSc dissertation (Interactive Media, 2020) focused on designing a mobile application for UCC ACCESS+, a programme supporting students from Cork City DEIS schools transitioning into Further Education. The project explored accessibility and access to higher education, particularly for underrepresented groups. User research showed that the app made information easier to find and more enjoyable to use than the existing website, highlighting how mobile design can improve awareness and engagement with educational support services.
